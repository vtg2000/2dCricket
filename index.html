Cricket!

The yellow circle is your bat. Click and drag it around to hit the ball.

The squares are the fielders.

  • White one - 0 runs
  • Light blue - 1 run
  • Blue - 2 runs
  • Green one - 3 runs
  • Red - Out!

    • The red rectange is the stumps

    Chase the target at the top right to win

    Press r to restart at any time
    Press d to play a quick game
